Steak Fajita

This is my own recipe that I made up

Ingredients
Sizzle Steak 2-3
Peppers 1-2
Onions 1-2
Cheese 1/2-1 Cup
Tortillas 4
Chili Pepepr As needed
Cumin As needed

  • Ingredients
  • Flat Pan or Griddle
  • Spatula
  • Non-stick cooking spray
  • Knife
  • Large pan

  1. Cook steak on stove or grill wiht seasoning sprinkled on each side
  2. Cut steak into strips or cubes
  3. At the same time you cook your steak place the peppers and onions in the large pan over heat to cook wiht seasoning
  4. Spray griddle or flat pan wiht non-stick cooking spray. Place one tortilla on griddle or flat pan
  5. Spread cheese around whole tortilla and place steak on top of cheese (place as much steak as your want)
  6. Place peppers and onions on top of steak and sprinkle more cheese on top of this
  7. Placce second tortilla on top of everything and flip the whole fajita using the spatula
  8. Keep flipping everyone 45 seconds until the tortilla is the color you like your tortilla
  9. Remove fajita from heat and place on a plate. Cut the fajita into triangles
  10. If you have enough for a second fajita and would like to make one repeat this same process
